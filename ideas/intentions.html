Intentions

I wrote these down as another way to help generate ideas. These are what I feel like I want software to help me accomplish in my life:
  • See the people I care about more
  • Keep my relationship exciting
  • Keep in touch with family
  • Connect more meaningfully with my community
  • Experience new and wonderful things
  • Live more mindfully
  • Become aware of my impact on the environment
  • Create tools that are useful and have a positive impact
  • Gain empathy for and understanding of people I don't know
  • Produce creative works that are fulfilling for me and enjoyable for others
